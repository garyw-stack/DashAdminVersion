Version 0.0.4 Notes

Point of Sale functions

Updates to Point of Sale functionality

  • Update Open and Close Shift button function to also open the Cash Drawer
  • Update Cash Drawer functionality to only open in the Event of Cash transactions only
  • Updated the Multiple Payments to autopopulate the remaining amount for the second payment

Receipt Layout updates

Visual updates to the receipt layout

  • Updated header to display information at header of receipt based on Location information
  • Updated product layout to display with quanity and value; as well as remove <single>U value
  • Updated Line items and Barcode Section to hide if has no value

Sales Entry update

Update to Sales Entry to include Charge Card amount in a non-edit field

Discount Updates

Update to Discount functionality to apply the correct Paid/Bonus Points when used. Also added a field in the Invoice section of Payment process

Minor bug Fixes

Minor bug fix

  • Minor bug fix to Cash Drawer functionality if Cash used first. Added "Change Owed" window to fix issue
  • Minor fix in Orders Page to Credit Card instead of cash during CC transactions
